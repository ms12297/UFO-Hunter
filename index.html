Ufo Hunter

Introduction

Welcome! My name is Zaeem Shahzad. This my first ever game built in p5.js showcased on a website. This is one of those games where you can never get tired of playing. Have fun shooting UFOs and obsessing over your highscore. I decided to make this game to master using objects in JavaScript. It reminds me a lot of a game I used to play about spaceships when I was younger.

Design

I started with just making a basic game with no graphics to nail the mechanics of the game. Later, I customized it to make it more aesthetically pleasing. What you see here is the final product, a website styled for the game. I hope you enjoy it as much as I did making it.

Instructions

Arrow keys to move the spaceship and SpaceBar to shoot the UFOs.

Avoid the asteroids!

Shoot the UFOs! 2 UFO Miss for Easy Mode, 1 for Medium, 0 for Hard allowed.
